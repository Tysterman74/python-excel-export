DCP-110 C

DCP-110 C is a compact color, inkjet printing device, additionally supporting copier and scanner functions. Crisp and clear color printing allows production of high quality pictures, while media card slots allow easy connectivity to digital camera. This provides a quick way to print digital photos. Maximum speed for monochrome print is 20 pages per minute and 15 pages per minute for color printing. DCP-110 C can hold 100 sheets and maximum monthly duty cycle can reach up to 2500 printed pages. Color scanner runs at resolutions of up to 19200 dpi. The device works with Windows and Mac operating systems and uses a standard USB 2.0 interface for connectivity.
